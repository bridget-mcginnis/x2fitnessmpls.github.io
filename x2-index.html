BRAZILIAN JIU-JITSU & KETTLEBELL TRAINING

X2 Fitness is a women-owned, community-focused gym with a strong female presence. We offer small group women-only classes in Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu and Kettlebells, as well as all-gender classes in Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu. Private instruction for Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu and Boxing is available upon request.

Our gym is hella fucking queer, but you don’t have to be queer to train here. If you’re not good with diversity, we’re not the gym for you. Dope AF people are welcome.

Owner & Head Instructor Gina Franssen, Minnesota’s first female black belt in Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, has over twenty years of experience training clients whose goals range from improving fitness to competing at a national level.
